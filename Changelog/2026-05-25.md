## Runesmith 2 2026-05-25 Changelog (v0.2.0)

### Balances
- Elemental Decay now give Elements equal to your Energy when exhausting X cost cards.
- Albus buffed: Craft an Albus Rune with 2(3) Charges ⇒ 3(4) Charges.
- Engraved Hammer buffed: Deal 6(9) damage ⇒ Deal 7(10) damage.
- Shock Therapy nerfed: Deal 6 damage to ALL enemies ⇒ Deal 5 damage.
- Fission Hammer adjusted: Deal 11(13) damage. Enhance 1(2) card by this card's Enhance amount ⇒ Deal 11(14) damage. Enhance 1 card by this card's Enhance amount plus 1(2).
- Lucky 7 reworked: Cost 2 Energy and 4|4|4 Elements - Recipe. Deal 35(49) damage to all enemies.
- Flowstate buffed: Now also draw 1 card.
- Fixed Teleport not having upgraded effect. (Upgraded: add 2 charges to the Rune)
- Complete Circuit nerfed: Deal 5(9) damage to ALL enemies ⇒ Deal 5(8) damage. 

### Content
- Added new visuals
  - Card Trail color
  - Rest Site character (Placeholder)
  - Beta art for cards. All 88 cards should have art now.

### Bug Fixes
- Fixed Prism rounding Elements loss incorrectly.
- Fixed Morbus Rune triggering at the end of turn instead of start of turn.
- Void Form and Brilliant Scarf should now make card with Elements cost free to play.
- Fixed Catalytic Converter not boosting Laser Turret's damage.
- Fixed Duplicate causing issue when played without Runes.
- Fixed Rune Hurl causing error when played too quickly for targeting to update (must now be played with a target even when you have no Runes.)
- Fixed exceptions caused by card trail.
