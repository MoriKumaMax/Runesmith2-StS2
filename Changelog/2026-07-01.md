## Runesmith 2 2026-07-01 Changelog (v0.7.5)

### Content and Balances
- Duct Tape power will no longer trigger for cards played by autoplay and Enhanced by Grindstone.
- (Mostly visual) Grindstone effect will not activate if the card is power or is autoplayed (for the last card in replay chain).
- Hot Hammer adjusted: Deal 8(10) ⇒ 6(9) damage. Enhance 1(2) ⇒ 2(2) cards at random.

### Bug Fixes
- Fixed some cards with Adroit Enchantment not being able to receive Enchantment (if the card didn't deal damage or gain Block by default.)
