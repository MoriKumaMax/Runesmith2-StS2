## Runesmith 2 2026-05-20 Changelog

### Balances
- Added keyword: Improved - Cards that are Upgraded, Enchanted, Enhanced, or Stasis are considered to be Improved.
- Augmented Body buffed: Now retain all Improved cards.
- Electromagnet buffed: Now pick any Improved cards.
- Resupply reworded: Now shuffle all Improved cards.
- Curtain of Flames buffed: Gain 5(7) Brace => Gain 6(8) Brace.
- Particle Accelerator buffed: Runes are Crafted with 1 additional charge => 2(4) additional charges. (No longer reduce cost on upgrade)

### Bug Fixes
- Updated mod manifest for newest game version.
- Fixed Augmented Body+ not functioning correctly when there are un-enhanceable cards in hand.
- Fixed Superposition damaging all enemies instead of one.
- Fixed Assembler not exhausting card.
- Fixed Vulcanus Rune not displaying Block number.
